About me

Electrical engineering was always around me growing up — my dad introduced me to it early, and it stuck. The more I looked into it, the more I realised how many directions it could take you. That's what drew me in.

Right now I'm building towards a career in control systems — ideally somewhere like Lockheed Martin — but I'm early in the journey and enjoying every bit of it. I learn best by doing, which is why I started taking on side projects before uni even got properly underway.

Outside of engineering I'm someone who likes people — I enjoy collaborating, debating, public speaking, and working as part of a team. If you're building something interesting and need someone who'll show up, dig in, and bring energy to the room, let's talk.
